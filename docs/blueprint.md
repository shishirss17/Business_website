# **App Name**: SnuggleShop

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Browse a wide variety of soft toys, categorized by type, age range, and brand.
- Product Details: View detailed information about each product, including images, descriptions, materials, and care instructions.
- Shopping Cart: Add products to a shopping cart and manage quantities.
- Secure Checkout: Process secure online payments.
- Order Tracking: Track the status of your order from placement to delivery.
- Personalized Recommendations: Suggest soft toys and gifts based on viewing history and purchase history.
- Gift Message Generation: AI tool generates a custom gift message based on recipient and occasion to include with order.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft pastel pink (#FFD1DC) for a comforting and friendly feel.
- Background color: Light off-white (#F8F8FF), offering a gentle contrast and calming effect.
- Accent color: Muted lavender (#E6E6FA), providing a gentle contrast that complements the pink.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a modern serif to provide a high-end feel; use 'PT Sans' for body.
- Use hand-drawn style icons for categories and interactive elements, for a cute, artisanal feeling.
- Clean, uncluttered layout with ample white space to showcase the products and promote a sense of calm.
- Subtle animations for product transitions and user interactions, adding a touch of delight without being distracting.
